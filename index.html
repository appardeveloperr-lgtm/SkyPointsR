Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

What is SkyPoints Rush?
SkyPoints Rush is an action-packed 3D mobile airplane game for Android where players fly through high-altitude tracks, dodge obstacles, and collect points.
How do I earn points?
You collect sky points by flying through glowing rings, hitting speed boosters, and completing daily challenge objectives.
Is the game free to play?
Yes! SkyPoints Rush is 100% free to download and play from the Google Play Store.
Does it work offline?
Yes, all core gameplay modes are completely accessible offline without requiring active Wi-Fi or cellular data.
How do I update the game?
Updates are released directly through the Google Play Store. Ensure auto-updates are enabled on your device for the latest features.
Where can I report issues or get support?
You can reach out directly using the Contact Support section below or via developer email at support@skypointsrush.com.
